Amazon Kindle Publishing and Large-Scale Evolutionary Optimization

25/02/2021
Hi everyone (well im assuming atleast one person will read this), so as a person I'm always thinking of ways I can make passive income (and to date I have no methods), but I found out that your can upload books to Amazon Kindle for free! (well Amazon get some royalities) So in short, I come from quite a creative family, my mothers a musician, dads and full-time artist my sister is like a wizzz on the Harp and Piano and plays for the UK National Youth Orchestra (link here). One of the things I think has gone un-noticed during this Pandemic, or even something that has been given not much attention is the affect this has had on children with learning difficulties, especially those who are young and dont understand whats going on. Soo, with all this creativity in my family I thought, what if we tried to write a short (very short) "childrens" book as stories that can maybe better explain to these young people, and even stories that could help them with growing up. Anyway that was my business idea for the day aha!
Secondly, Large-Scale Evolutionary Optimization, is the optimization of high-dimensional problems. So a method that is mostly used in the literature split the large objective vector into smaller discrete groups and try to optimize each "chunk" of the large-vector on its own. My thought is, what is different dimensions of the problem share similar characteristics? That is, it may be really large problem, but maybe the shape of different dimensions are the same or even have similar optimums for the problem? I wonder if anyone has thought of applying a Multi-Task Evolutionary Algorithm to the decomposed problem instead of the Coevolutionary method that most decomposing algorithms use? Anyway, thats my two ideas for today, until next time, Hwyl Fawr!
